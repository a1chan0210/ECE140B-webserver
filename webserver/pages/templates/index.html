Current Project Idea: Pep Pets

Team: Anthony Chan, Kathy Li, Kendrick Nguyen, Alex Hernandez-Perez

Our project, Pep Pets, is a digital handheld pet marketed towards young children around ages 8 - 13. It is a digital pet that needs to be taken care of and incentivizes children to become more active, social and learn about the responsibilities of raising a pet.

Who is it for?

Pep Pets is designed for children around the ages 8-13. It gives children around that age another way to play and socialize with each other. Pep Pets is also a great way to help parents teach their children responsibilities for taking care of pets as well as caring for others.

What it does

Pep Pets is an alternative to mobile games for children to have fun and interact with friends. It is a digital pet that you can take care of by playing with it, being active outside, socializing with friends, and taking care of it.

How it works

Pep pets has a happiness bar and tiredness bar that needs to be taken care of. This can be done by completing tasks, taking care of the pet, feeding it, etc. All actions that take care of the pet will change the levels of the happiness and tiredness bars. Taking care of the Pep Pet will accumulate experience points which will level up the Pep Pet and unlock customizations. Interacting with friends will increase the accumlated experience points.

Key Benefits

The benefits of Pep Pets is that we provide your children with a safe and engaging way to have fun and socialize. Children will be incentived to go outside and be more active while taking care of their Pep Pet.

Our Current Product Mock-Up

Our Overall Concept Design

The pep pet is planned to be a small handheld device that children will take care of. It will have indicators for hunger and happiness as well as an experience bar to show growth. There will be buttons to be able to interact with it in ways such as feeding the pet, putting it to sleep, and other activities.
