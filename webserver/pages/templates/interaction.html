Key Product Interactions

Pep Pet Interactions

Pep Pets can interact with other pep pets by physically connecting and increase the experience and happiness that they gain to encourage socializing with friends and their pep pets.

Parent Website

The parents have access to a website that can keep track of their child's Pep pet progress and friends that they make to see how active their children are and how much they socialize with their friends.

Minimalist Design

The pep pet will have a simple design with only 3 buttons and a simplet interface. This will make the Pep Pet easy to use and easy to understand how it works.

Experience Levels

The Pep Pet will display an experience bar and acuumulated levels to show a sense of prgoression and goals to progress further.
