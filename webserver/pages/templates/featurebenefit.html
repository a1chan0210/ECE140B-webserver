Features vs Benefits

Features Benefits
Responds to physical activity by tracking steps. This benefits parents by encouraging their children to be more active and play outside.
Interacts with other pep pets. This benefits children by creating another way to socialize with their friends.
Pep Pets are small and hand held. This benefits kids so they can easily take Pep Pets anywhere with them.
Pep Pets need to be taken care of like with feeding, walking, etc. This benefits parents by teaching their children a sense of responsibility to take care of others.
The Pep Pet will level up over time. This benefits kids by making the Pep Pet more engaging to kids and shows growth over time.
